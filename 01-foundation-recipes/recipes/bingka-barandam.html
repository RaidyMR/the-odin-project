Bingka Barandam

How To Make

Bingka Barandam, a trademark of the Banjar tribe in South Kalimantan, has a sweet, rich, and soft taste.

Ingridients

  • 3 chicken eggs
  • 5 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • A pinch of salt
  • A small amount of turmeric powder (as a yellow coloring substitute)

ðŸ«— Sauce Ingredients:

  • 500 ml water
  • 75 grams of granulated sugar
  • 1 pandan leaf
  • A small amount of turmeric powder

Step-by-Step

  1. In a bowl, mix the eggs and vanilla, then beat at high speed until fluffy and frothy.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and turmeric powder. Stir gently with a spatula until well combined.
  3. Heat the mold, grease with margarine. Pour in the batter, cook over low heat, and cover. Once cooked, remove and set aside. Repeat until all the batter is used.
  4. For the sauce: mix all the sauce ingredients, wait until boiling and fragrant with pandan. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Serve the Bingka on a plate with the sauce. Bingka Barandam is ready to be enjoyed~~
