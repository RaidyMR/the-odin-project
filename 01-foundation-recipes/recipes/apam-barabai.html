Apam Barabai

How To Make

Apam Barabai is a traditional food from South Kalimantan made from rice flour and brown sugar.

Ingridients

  • Grated coconut for topping
  • 2 pandan leaves, knotted
  • 200 ml palm sugar syrup / Javanese sugar syrup
  • 100 ml water
  • 200 ml coconut milk (such as Santan Kara brand)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Dough ingredients
  • 110 grams rice flour
  • 110 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 3 tablespoons cassava tape

Step-by-Step

  1. Boil coconut milk, palm sugar, pandan leaves, and salt. Remove from heat and set aside. Steam grated coconut.
  2. Mix cassava tape into the cooled coconut milk mixture. Gradually add rice flour and all-purpose flour, then yeast.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour. Pour into greased molds and steam until cooked.
  4. Serve the apam cakes with steamed grated coconut.
