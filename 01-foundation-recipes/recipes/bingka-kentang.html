Bingka Kentang

How To Make

Bingka kentang, a trademark of the Banjar tribe in South Kalimantan, has a sweet, rich, and soft taste.

Ingridients

  • 250 grams of granulated sugar
  • 250 grams of steamed potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 200 grams of rice flour
  • 75 grams of wheat flour
  • 1 pandan leaf
  • 1 liter of coconut milk, extracted
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla powder
  • Margarine (for greasing)

Step-by-Step

  1. Cook coconut milk, salt, pandan leaf, and vanilla until boiling, then simmer.
  2. Reduce mixture to 600 ml, strain, and set aside.
  3. Whisk sugar and eggs, then add mashed potatoes and mix.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake at 180°C for 45 minutes.
  5. Cool, then remove from pan and serve.
