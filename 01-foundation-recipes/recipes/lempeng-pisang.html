Ingridients

  • 5-6 bananas
  • 3 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 5 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of melted margarine
  • 5 tablespoons of palm sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • Some water/coconut milk

Step-by-Step

  1. Put the flour in a bowl, then add the grated coconut, palm sugar, and salt.
  2. Mix the batter well, then add the mashed bananas and melted margarine (mix well).
  3. Heat a pan, add a little margarine, then pour in the batter. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip.
  4. Top with condensed milk and cheese, if desired.
